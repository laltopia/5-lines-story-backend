7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption: HTTPS encryption for all data in transit
  • Database security: Encrypted at rest, access controls
  • Authentication: Secure JWT-based authentication
  • Rate limiting: Protection against abuse and attacks
  • Security headers: Content Security Policy, XSS protection
  • Regular updates: Security patches and monitoring

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

To exercise these rights, email privacy@storymaking.ai

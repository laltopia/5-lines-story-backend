Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change plans anytime?
Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time through your account settings. Changes take effect immediately.
What happens when I reach my story limit?
On the Free plan, you'll need to wait until next month or upgrade to Pro for unlimited stories. We'll send you a notification when you're close to your limit.
Is there a refund policy?
Yes! We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans. No questions asked.
Do you offer discounts for annual billing?
Yes! Save 20% when you pay annually. Contact us for annual billing options.
How do I manage my subscription?
Click on your profile icon in the top right corner and select "Manage subscription" to view, upgrade, or cancel your plan through Clerk.
